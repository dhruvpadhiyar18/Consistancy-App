## ğŸ”’ Privacy & Data Integrity
**Your data is truly yours.** We believe your learning journey is private and personal.

*   **100% Local Storage:** Everything is stored on your device using Android SharedPreferences.
*   **No Accounts:** No login, no signup, no email required.
